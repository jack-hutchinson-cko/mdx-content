Here you'll find an explanation of the various fraud codes you might encounter when processing Visa- and Mastercard-branded cards.

---

## Visa fraud codes

<Table>
<TableHead headers={['Code', 'Reason', 'Description']} />
<TableBody>
<TableRow>
<TableCell>

0

</TableCell>
<TableCell>

Card reported lost

</TableCell>
<TableCell>

A fraudulent payment that occurs with the use of a lost card, without
the actual, implied, or apparent authority of the cardholder

</TableCell>
</TableRow>
<TableRow>
<TableCell>

1

</TableCell>
<TableCell>

Stolen

</TableCell>
<TableCell>

A fraudulent payment that occurs with the use of a stolen card, without
the actual, implied, or apparent authority of the cardholder

</TableCell>
</TableRow>
<TableRow>
<TableCell>

2

</TableCell>
<TableCell>

Not received as issued (NRI)

</TableCell>
<TableCell>

The interception and use of a card before receipt by a cardholder or by
a person without the actual, implied, or apparent authority of the
cardholder

</TableCell>
</TableRow>
<TableRow>
<TableCell>

3

</TableCell>
<TableCell>

Fraudulent application

</TableCell>
<TableCell>

A fraudulent payment that occurs with the use of a card that was
obtained with an application using a false name or other false
identification information

</TableCell>
</TableRow>
<TableRow>
<TableCell>

4

</TableCell>
<TableCell>

Issuer counterfeit

</TableCell>
<TableCell>

The use of an altered or illegally-reproduced card

</TableCell>
</TableRow>
<TableRow>
<TableCell>

5

</TableCell>
<TableCell>

Miscellaneous

</TableCell>
<TableCell>

Any type of fraud which is not listed under the codes 0 - 4 or 6 and 9
below.

</TableCell>
</TableRow>
<TableRow>
<TableCell>

6

</TableCell>
<TableCell>

Fraudulent use of account number

</TableCell>
<TableCell>

A fraudulent payment that occurs with the use of card information
including pseudo-account information without the physical card or other
device being involved, via the phone, mail, Internet, or other
electronic means without the actual, implied, or apparent authority of
the cardholder

</TableCell>
</TableRow>
<TableRow>
<TableCell>

9

</TableCell>
<TableCell>

Acquirer counterfeit

</TableCell>
<TableCell>

A fraud arising out of collusion between a merchant and the acquirer
bank, without the actual, implied or apparent authority of the
cardholder

</TableCell>
</TableRow>
<TableRow>
<TableCell>

A

</TableCell>
<TableCell>

Incorrect processing

</TableCell>
<TableCell>

Issuer confirms a payment is fraudulent on a Visa account as a result of
incorrect processing; such as fraud on a VbV payment that was
incorrectly identified as a secure e-commerce payment as a result of
incorrect CAVV

</TableCell>
</TableRow>
<TableRow>
<TableCell>

B

</TableCell>
<TableCell>

Account or credentials takeover

</TableCell>
<TableCell>

Issuer confirms a payment is fraudulent on a Visa account as a result of
fraudulent payments resulted from a fraudster taking over an account or its credentials

</TableCell>
</TableRow>
</TableBody>
</Table>
