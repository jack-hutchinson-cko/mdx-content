---
title: 3D Secure test cards
account: NAS
---

Use the test cards on this page to simulate different 3D Secure (3DS) authentication flows and results. You can use any valid CVV and any future expiry date (mm/yy).

When testing 3DS authentication, you will receive a redirect URL to our 3DS simulator page. The password for the simulator is `Checkout1!`.
